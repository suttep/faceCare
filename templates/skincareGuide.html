Here is your skincare recommendation
Morning Routine
Facial Wash
Look for facial wash that cleanse gently. don't invest much on the extra ingredient. Invest the extra ingredient on serums or moisturizer.
Vitamin C
Look for vitamin C (L-Ascorbic Acid) that also has vitamin E and Ferulic Acid. But the important ingredient is the vitamin C itself.
Moisturizer
In the morning you can use Moisturizer that also has SPF 30+ or above. But if buy moisturizer that have SPF 30+ in it, you can't use it at night. So you have to buy 2 different moisturizer. look for hydrating ingredient and ceramide.
Sunscreen
Look for SPF30+ or above, also look for the rating of the sunscreen. Look sunscreen that protects from UVA and UVB. Usually if the sunscreen is good, they have "Broad Spectrum" rating or "PA+++" or above rating.
Night Routine
Facial Wash
Look for facial wash that cleanse gently. don't invest much on the extra ingredient. Invest the extra ingredient on serums or moisturizer.
{% if skinType == 2 %}
Exfoliant
Look for "Lactic Acid" in the ingredient. Don't buy exfoliant that uses Scrub. AHA & BHA exfoliant also helps. Try to look ingredient that also hydrate your face.
{% else %}
Exfoliant
Look for AHA BHA exfoliant. Don't buy exfoliant that uses Scrub. Salicylic acid also helps with your skin condition.
{% endif %}
Serums
Niacinamide
Look for low percentage Niacinamide. 5% Niacinamide is already good and beneficial to your skin. Higher dose can damage your skin if your skin never interact with Niacinamide before.
Retinol
If you are under 18, pregnant or nursing don't use this, see retinol alternative. For a beginner we recommend look for low dose retinol first because if you use advanced retinol it can damage your skin. We recommend to layer your retinol. First use moisturizer, then retinol, then moisturizer again.
Retinol Alternative
A good alternative for retinol is Bakuchiol. It has almost the same benefit for retinol and most bakuchiol is vegan.
Moisturizer
If your morning moisturizer does not contain sunscreen (SPF). then you can use your morning moisturizer. Else, if your morning moisturizer contain sunscreen. You need to buy another moisturizer. Look for hydrating ingredient and ceramide.
